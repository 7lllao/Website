Zhao Zhou is a speculative designer working primarily with immaterial mediums such as air. Through their practice, they use speculative design as a vehicle to investigate the ideologies and ethics surrounding our urban environment and its future trajectory. They challenge traditional notions of urban design and survey underutilized senses to evoke alternate interpretations in experience and behavior. The work induces turbulence in space, presenting an uncanny contradiction as the structures appear industrial and distant, yet they are alive and seek intimacy. This transformative approach changes the flow of a space and reshapes one’s relation to it.
