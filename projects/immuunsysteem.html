Immuunsysteem

Forced Switch Series

The interactive installation utilizes an aluminum grid suspended from the ceiling, measuring 1.6m x 1.6m. The grid carries 16 objects that produce air vortexes at each crossing point and is equipped with sensors that detect the presence of visitors. The installation adapts its behavior based on the presence of visitors. When no one is present, it operates in a low-pressure state, randomly activating one object at random intervals. However, when someone enters its private space, the installation responds by going into high-pressure and rapidly activating all the objects, altering the sense of space and atmosphere.
